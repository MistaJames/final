What is Mindy's Bridal and Dress Shop?

Mindy's Bridal and Dress Shop is privately owned and operated for over 15 years. We order from a variety of dress designers, and also special made dresses from your own designs and concepts.

What do you sell?

We sell bridal dresses, as well as bridesmaids, prom, special occasions, and flower girl dresses. We also sell bridal accessories, such as shoes, veils, and crowns.

How do I order dresses?

We take the customer's measurements and the down payment to take the orders.

Do you do any additional services?

We do in-store alterations and shoe dyes.

What about orders over long distance (via phone and/or Internet)?

We can also take your order by your measurements.

How can pay for my order?

We accept cash, check, and all major credit cards.
